Example 1: Game Page "How to Play" Section

Paragraph + Numbered List
Target Query: "How to play Moto X3M"
SERP Feature: Featured snippet (list)
Expected Position: Position 0 (above rank 1)

HTML Implementation

<section class="game-section snippet-optimized how-to-play">
    <h2>How to Play Moto X3M</h2>
    <p>Master Moto X3M with these simple steps to get started immediately:</p>

    <ol class="snippet-list">
        <li><strong>Load the Game:</strong> Click the Play button above to load Moto X3M in your browser.</li>
        <li><strong>Learn the Controls:</strong> Review the control instructions displayed on screen (Arrow keys or WASD).</li>
        <li><strong>Understand Your Goal:</strong> Read the level objective shown at the start of each level.</li>
        <li><strong>Start Playing:</strong> Click Start or press the designated key to begin the first level.</li>
        <li><strong>Master Basic Mechanics:</strong> Practice fundamental actions like acceleration, steering, and landing jumps in early levels.</li>
        <li><strong>Use Speed Strategically:</strong> Build momentum on straightaways but control deceleration on curves.</li>
        <li><strong>Complete Each Level:</strong> Finish the level objective to unlock new challenges and progress through the game.</li>
        <li><strong>Improve Your Time:</strong> Replay levels to beat your personal best time and achieve faster completion speeds.</li>
    </ol>
</section>

Example 4: Game Tips & Tricks Section

Numbered List
Target Query: "Moto X3M tips and tricks"
SERP Feature: Featured snippet (list)
Expected Position: Position 0

HTML Implementation

<section class="game-section snippet-optimized tips-tricks">
    <h2>Moto X3M Tips and Tricks</h2>
    <p>Master Moto X3M and achieve high scores with these pro strategies:</p>

    <ol class="tips-list">
        <li><strong>Angle Your Bike Before Landings:</strong> Tilt your bike
        backward before landing jumps to prevent flipping and maintain balance through
        landing zones.</li>

        <li><strong>Study Track Patterns:</strong> Watch the track layout and
        obstacle positions during preview. Memorize jump angles, landing zones, and
        turning points for faster completion.</li>

        <li><strong>Use Wheelies for Shortcuts:</strong> Execute wheelies on
        flat sections to discover hidden paths and find faster routes through levels,
        reducing completion time significantly.</li>

        <li><strong>Build Momentum on Straightaways:</strong> Accelerate fully
        on straight sections to gain speed, then control deceleration carefully on
        curves to maintain traction.</li>

        <li><strong>Practice Early Levels:</strong> Perfect your skills on
        the first 5-10 levels before attempting harder stages. Mastering fundamentals
        is essential for later progression.</li>

        <li><strong>Customize Control Sensitivity:</strong> Adjust your bike's
        sensitivity settings in the options menu to match your playstyle—aggressive
        for stunts or conservative for stability.</li>

        <li><strong>Watch Replay High Scores:</strong> Study replays of players
        with top times to learn advanced techniques, optimal jump angles, and shortcut
        combinations you haven't discovered yet.</li>
    </ol>
</section>
Impact: 706 featured snippets (one per game)
Implementation Time: 4-5 hours
Risk Level: Very Low
