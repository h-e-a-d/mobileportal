Instructions

In the distant past, the great castle lost its grandeur and became an abandoned place. Legends said that the treasures of the Alogons were hidden inside the castle. One day, a group of archaeologists decided to explore the castle and uncover its secrets. They set off on a dangerous journey through the intricate corridors and dark caves of the castle. After several hours of research, they came across a secret room that contained an ancient treasure chest. However, the chest was closed and placed in a cage to protect it from outsiders. The archaeologists had to use all their knowledge and dexterity to solve the puzzles that guarded the chest
