Instructions

The Hugli Wugli brothers are lost in the castle, and in the first game of the series, you must help them. These mischievous brothers have strapped hoverboards to their feet and are trying to escape from the castle. But beware -- the castle is full of traps, and you need to be careful! Find the red and blue keys as quickly as possible. Watch out for the zombie in the castle -- if you get caught, you lose.
