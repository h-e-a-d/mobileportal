Instructions

Quick Shot is a fast-paced online shooting game where precision is everything. Your goal is simple: hit each target exactly in the center to score the highest points. But be careful — you can only miss five times before the game ends. With quick reflexes and sharp aim, you’ll need to stay focused as the targets appear faster and more unpredictably. How long can you keep your streak alive? Test your skills and aim for perfection in Quick Shot!
