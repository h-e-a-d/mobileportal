Instructions

Are you ready for the adventure of Dog and Cat? A super exhausting journey awaits you. There are obstacles everywhere, and you must be very careful. The cat and dog must be like siblings. They need to work together as a team; otherwise, they wont reach the doghouse at the finish line. They must collect all the food they find on the way because the cat and dog must not go hungry. Gather all the food you find, watch out for obstacles, cross the bridge and the iron gate, and reach the doghouse.
