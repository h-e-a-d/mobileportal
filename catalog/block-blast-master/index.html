About Block Blast Master

Block Blast Master Step into the world of strategy and precision in this addictive block puzzle game! Start by selecting a block from the set and simply drag and drop it onto the board. Choose each position wisely—every move can shape the outcome of your game. Create complete horizontal or vertical lines to clear them from the board and earn valuable points. The more lines you complete, the more space you free up for new blocks! Think ahead and plan your moves carefully. Anticipate which shapes might come next and leave room for future placements—smart planning is key to mastering the game. The game ends when there’s no space left to place a new block. How long can you keep playing? Challenge yourself to score as high as possible!
