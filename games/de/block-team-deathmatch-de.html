Instructions

Block Team Deathmatch is a first-person shooting simulation game. All models are made of pixel blocks including scenes, characters, and weapons. We have 4v4 Team Mode, Individual Mode, and Sniper Mode for you to select. If I were you, I would play the Team Mode first for better equipment, then challenge the other 2 modes when the rank levels up. If you never played any shooting games, I recommend you buy an auto-rifle to fight at any distance. Keep calm and shoot first!
