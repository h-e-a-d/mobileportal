Instructions

In this new era, all national leagues have been removed and now all teams play in 4 international divisions! Have you ever thought of creating and managing a club? Create your own football club and lead it towards glory in Football Manager Simulator! Manage your squad, tactics, and finances, compete in leagues and climb to the top division, sign football stars and coach your players to make them better! However, the road to glory wont be easy... Youll need to earn the money for coaching, signing players, and academy scouting. Upgrade your stadium to win more money on home matches and get sponsors to earn crucial amounts of money!
