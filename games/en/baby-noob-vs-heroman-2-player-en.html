Instructions

Baby Noob and Heroman are lost in the forest. They need to reach home safely. Baby Noob is a master at unlocking chests, so he must open all the chests in the forest and find the mysterious key hidden deep in the forest at a house. Although Heroman is fearless, the monsters are huge and enormous, so he must be very careful. To defeat the monsters, he should use his sword. Some houses have iron doors, and he must use his sword to break those doors. Collect all the diamonds and find the key.
