Instructions

In this adventure, Obby and his girlfriend need to complete a skyblock obstacle course that seems easy but is actually quite challenging. They must be careful and never, ever separate from each other. If they do, they could die. Both of them need to reach the finish line safely. They must be very cautious as there are obstacles everywhere. The course features spiked traps on the ground and moving obstacles that go up and down. Help Obby and his girlfriend reach the finish line.
