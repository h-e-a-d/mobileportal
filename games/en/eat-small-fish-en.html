Instructions

Eat Small Fish is a game where you must eat small other fish to survive and become bigger. Eat Small Fish include all kinds of hungry fishes in the undersea water. They tanked and attacked smaller fish to become the ocean most powerful fish eater. Big fish eat small fish to be giant fish. Giant fish eat big fish and turn over the Mega fish. Mega fish eat giant fish and finally become fabulous fish in the underwater sea.
