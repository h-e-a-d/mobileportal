Geometry Rash is a high-speed action platformer where every move counts. Jump your way through 8 challenging levels filled with spikes and traps. As you progress, you’ll unlock new gameplay forms, fly with the ship or boost with the rocket. Mastering each style is key to surviving later levels. Along the way, collect coins to unlock fresh character looks in the shop. With tight controls and tough level design, this game will test your reflexes and rhythm!
