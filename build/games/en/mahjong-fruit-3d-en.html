Instructions

Before you on the screen will be seen a three-dimensional image of cubes on the faces of which you will see various images of fruits. You will need to carefully examine everything, find the same fruits completely and clicking on them to highlight the cubes on which they are applied. Having done this, you will see how these objects will disappear from the game field. For this, you will accrue points and you will make the next move. Your task in the game Mahjong Fruit 3D in the minimum time and the number of moves completely clean the field of cubes.
