Instructions

Toy Claw Simulator is a fun and addictive arcade game where you take control of a virtual claw machine to collect a wide variety of adorable toys! Carefully aim and time your claw to grab plushies, action figures, and surprise collectibles. Once youve stocked up your toy inventory, head over to the factory to sell them for shiny coins! Use your earnings to run the machine to use the crane to unlock new toys. Its a charming blend of skill, strategy, and rewarding progression!
