Instructions

In this adventurous game, the stick huggies, the huggy brothers, need to reach the finish line. The red and blue huggies must run very fast to reach the finish line. You should collect the red and blue hands along the way. Collect all the hands on the road and reach the black portal at the finish line. You must reach the portal safely before time runs out. Both friends need to reach the finish line.
