Instructions

In this adventure, Steve joined Noob to help him, and they embarked on a joint quest together. Noob collected all the gold to create the key. Steve has the ability to see invisible levers, allowing him to activate and reveal invisible bridges. He can also open iron-locked doors. Because of this, Noob needs Steves assistance. Together, Noob and Steve must first reach the chest. Once they reach and open the chest, the invisible portal becomes visible, and they can access it.
