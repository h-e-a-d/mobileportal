Instructions

In this adventure, two siblings need to find all the necessary items. Each sibling must locate their own special key. After finding the keys, they must reach the chests. Before finding the keys and chests, they need to collect all the green coins along the way. The siblings must survive the challenges of the forest and reach the finish line safely. At the end, a floating portal awaits them.
