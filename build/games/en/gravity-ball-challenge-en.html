Instructions

Embark on an exciting journey with Gravity Ball Adventures, a 2D physics-based puzzle game that puts you in control of a charming, bouncy ball. Your mission is to navigate through a series of challenging levels filled with intricate traps, obstacles, and pitfalls to reach the hoop at the end of each stage. Use your unique ability to manipulate gravity--turn it on and off--to overcome the obstacles in your path. Timing and precision are key as you flip gravity to avoid deadly spikes, dodge moving platforms, and slide past tricky barriers. Each level presents a new puzzle to solve, testing your reflexes and strategic thinking.
