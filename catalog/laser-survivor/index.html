About Laser Survivor

Laser Survivor Laser Survivor, a cyberpunk roguelike action-adventure set in a neon-drenched post-apocalypse, the last hope for humanity rests in the hands of five heroes fighting hordes of demons pouring through a dimensional rift. Featuring pixel art visuals, randomized weapons, chiptune soundtracks, and relentless survival gameplay, every run is a test of adaptation and skill. Story The world has fallen. A dimensional rift has unleashed an unstoppable flood of demons, tearing through what remains of civilization. But from the chaos, five warriors rise. Each wielding unique powers forged for survival in this cyberpunk hellscape. Dropped at random into corrupted maps, they must adapt with whatever weapons and passive abilities they find, carving their path through waves of enemies, unlocking upgrades, and uncovering the truth behind the rift. The fate of Earth depends on them. Heroes Blaze: Fires homing bullets that lock onto the nearest enemy, ensuring no demon can escape. Neon: Projects a protective energy shield that damages anything foolish enough to get close. Hunter: Drops explosives that create deadly zones, controlling the battlefield with traps. Slash: Slices enemies apart with razor-sharp horizontal cuts that pierce entire hordes. Jaxx: A cyber-brute with massive, scaling area damage, crushing everything in his path. Features Cyberpunk Roguelike Gameplay: Every run is unique, with randomized spawn points, weapons, and abilities. 5 Maps to Conquer: Battle through the Lab, Forest, Crypt, Factory, and Dungeon, each swarming with demon hordes. 10 Active Weapons & 10 Passive Abilities: Build your arsenal on the fly as you adapt to randomized loadouts. 8 Missions & 11 Upgrades: Progress through challenging missions, earn permanent upgrades, and unlock new survival tactics. Collectibles & Lore: Discover secrets that reveal the origins of the rift and the story behind the invasion. Retro Pixel Art: Neon-infused visuals with hand-crafted pixel art and gameplay by the Felicity team. 80s Chiptune Soundtrack: Pulsating tracker music by Drozerix, driving the action with nostalgic energy. Why Play Laser Survivor? Fast-paced roguelike survival set in a sci-fi cyberpunk apocalypse. Play as five unique heroes, each with distinct base abilities and upgrade paths. Endless replayability with randomized runs and evolving builds. Perfect for fans of pixel art shooters, bullet-hell roguelikes, and retro-inspired chiptune soundtracks. Laser Survivor is more than survival. It’s humanity’s last stand in a cyberpunk post-apocalypse. Will you master the chaos, close the rift, and save the world... or be consumed by the demons?

How to Play Laser Survivor

  1. Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character
  2. Press spacebar or click to attack enemies
  3. Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities
  4. Avoid obstacles and enemy attacks
  5. Complete objectives to progress through levels
