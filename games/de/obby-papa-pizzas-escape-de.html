Instructions

In this adventure, Obby and Bacon are doing everything they can to survive and escape from the pizza place. Help Obby and Bacon escape from the pizza place with your friend. Be careful, as Chef Papa Pizza is very angry and wants to catch you. Watch out for the pizza maker--if they catch you, you cant escape and youll lose. Be very careful, as there are obstacles everywhere and the pizza maker is very angry. Bacon can defeat the pizza maker with his sword by throwing it at the pizza maker at the right time. Obby and Bacon need to collect all the pizzas and reach the red door to escape from the pizza maker
