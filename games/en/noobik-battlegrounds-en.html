Instructions

The tactical shooter Noobik Battlegrounds is waiting for you in its ranks, fighter. Online game in which you appear at a random point on the map, and then collect weapons and equipment, after which you kill the other players on map along the way. Game tasks: 1) Find weapons, equipment. 2) Gradually the zone will narrow to the center, you need to go to the center, otherwise you will be killed by zone that narrows. 3) There will be other survivors on your way, you will need to kill them all to win. 4) If you are killed, you can either watch other survivors or find a new session. 5) Winner is the one who remains the only one alive on map.
