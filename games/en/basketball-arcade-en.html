Instructions

In this game, you will grab your favorite basketball and will try to score as many points as possible. All of the free throws will happen from different angles and distances. Pick your favorite team and try to be the best player around! There is a time limit, so you need to be quick. You have a total of 20 balls that you can use. It’s no easy task, since you need to guess the angle and strength that you need to hit. How to play: - Pick up the ball over the table. - Press and hold left mouse button, when shooting, to increase the shooting force. Force will show on the right indicator. - Shoot and try to get the most score before time is over
