Instructions

The gameplay is simple: you slide the numbered tiles in any of the eight directions, and when two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one. Slide the Same Numbers offers a clean and minimalist design, with simple graphics and soothing sound effects. It is a game that will test your logical thinking and strategy skills, providing a challenging and engaging gameplay experience.
