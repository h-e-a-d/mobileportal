Instructions

Bridge Fun Race is an IO battle arcade game like Mr Bean series. With 3D cute roles, you can join different knockout battles with several AI opponents. Only the first 3 places players can win and join into the next battle. In Racing levels, you need to collect items and push the obstacles to the finish line. In Survival levels, you need to roll and launch snowballs to hit the other players onto the platform in a limited time.
