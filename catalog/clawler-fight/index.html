About Clawler Fight

Clawler Fight Immerse yourself in the thrilling blend of addictive gameplay of Clawler fight! In this action-packed game, you step into the shoes of an ordinary guy on a mission to survive the zombie apocalypse while tackling challenging levels. As you advance, you will collect valuable resources and currency, unlocking powerful upgrades for your character through a card-based skill progression system.
