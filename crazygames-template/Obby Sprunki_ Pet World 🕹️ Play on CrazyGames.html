Obby Sprunki: Pet World is an epic adventure where each pet is more than a companion. They're the key to unlocking legendary kingdoms filled with secrets, challenges, and hidden treasures. Journey through ever-changing environments, forge deep bonds with your pets, and uncover the ancient forces that shape the world.

Features

  • Dynamic challenges with traps, puzzles, and obstacles that change every playthrough
  • Mythic progression as pets grow stronger and wiser, the more you journey together
  • Solo mastery with levels that test your skill, creativity, and connection with your pets
  • Mystical synergy by combining pet abilities to burn, freeze, and summon powerful winds
  • Timeless mysteries waiting to be uncovered through ancient scripts and celestial puzzles

Controls

  • WASD = move
  • Left-click = interact with in-game buttons
  • Tab = show / hide mouse cursor
  • I = inventory menu
  • V = gift menu
  • T = teleport
  • E = interact with the objects
  • F = unlock the gate
