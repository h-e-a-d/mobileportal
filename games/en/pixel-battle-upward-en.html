Instructions

In this adventure, we must avoid the lava and push our enemy into it. Are you ready for a fun game of avoiding the lava with your friend? Whoever falls into the lava loses. To avoid falling into the lava, you must evade your friend. Run away from your friend or push them into the lava. Watch out for rockets falling from the sky. These rockets can kill you. The player who falls into the lava or dies the most loses the game. Play with your friend and try to beat them.
